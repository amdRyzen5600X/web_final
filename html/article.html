Today's news

Weekly report on the work done by the Student Government Good day to all, dear students! ✨

We are glad to welcome you and present to you the results of our activities over the past week (June 5-11). Plans for the year

Each of the committees is developing its own plan for the upcoming academic year. Recruitment of volunteers for graduation We have developed and launched a form for recruiting volunteers who will help graduates of our university at the graduation ceremony. Now we are looking for guys who will be in the city on July 1 and want to help us with the ceremony.

Decision on the dress code

This week was spent in discussions with both students and the university administration regarding the issue of the dress code. The result of these dialogues was the decision to allow wearing shorts at the university, while observing reasonable length limits. The change was made in accordance with the climatic conditions and the needs of students in comfortable clothes, so we hope that this will contribute to the comfort and self-expression of students. However, it is important to remember that shorts should be of adequate length so as not to create additional inconvenience or distract attention from the academic environment.
