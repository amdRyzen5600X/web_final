Sport in AITU

Promoting a healthy lifestyle is integral to the educational ethos at Astana IT University, where sports serve as a constructive alternative to detrimental habits. Recognizing the pivotal role of physical education in shaping students' well-being, we consider it an essential component of the learning journey. At Astana IT University, we actively nurture an environment that supports and encourages students in adopting an active lifestyle. We provide ample opportunities for engagement in various sports, fostering both individual and team activities. Our commitment extends to facilitating participation in diverse sports competitions and events at different levels. By integrating sports seamlessly into the educational experience, we aim to instill not only academic knowledge but also a holistic approach to health and well-being among our students.
